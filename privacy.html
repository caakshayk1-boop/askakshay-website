Privacy Policy

Finance Suite Apps  ·  Last updated: June 7, 2026  ·  Effective: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the following mobile applications published by Akshay Kothari ("we", "us", "our"):
  • Retail FP&A Toolkit (com.askakshay.fpnatoolkit)
  • Wealth Tracker (com.askakshay.wealthtracker)
  • Gold Trading Journal (com.askakshay.goldjournal)
  • AI Resume Builder (com.askakshay.resumebuilder)
  • AI Meeting Notes (com.askakshay.meetingnotes)

By using any of these apps, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the apps.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (hashed — never stored in plain text) via Firebase Authentication.

App data you create. We store the data you enter in the apps, including:

  • Financial calculations, KPI data, and margin figures (FP&A Toolkit)
  • Net worth snapshots, asset/liability values, and savings goals (Wealth Tracker)
  • Trade entries, P&L records, and journal notes (Gold Trading Journal)
  • Resume content, work history, and job application records (AI Resume Builder)
  • Meeting notes and action items (AI Meeting Notes)

Usage data. We collect anonymised analytics events (screen views, feature usage, login/signup actions) via Firebase Analytics to understand how the apps are used and improve them. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events.

Crash reports. When the app crashes, we collect a crash report including the error message, stack trace, and device model via Firebase Crashlytics. This helps us fix bugs. No personal financial data is included in crash reports.

Device information. We automatically collect basic device information such as operating system version, device model, and app version for diagnostic purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the apps and their features
  • To store your data securely and sync it across your devices
  • To authenticate you and protect your account
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To send you important service-related communications (e.g., password reset emails)
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription via RevenueCat

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database hosted by Google LLC. Data is stored in the asia-south1 region (Mumbai, India). Firebase provides industry-standard security including encryption at rest and in transit.

Access to your data is restricted by security rules: only you can read or write your own data. No other user can access your account data.

We implement security measures including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), authentication tokens, and server-side access control rules. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Remote Config, Analytics) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchase management) — RevenueCat Privacy Policy. RevenueCat processes your purchase history to manage your subscription entitlements.
  • Anthropic (AI features in AI Resume Builder) — When you use the AI resume optimization feature, your resume text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate improved content. No data is retained by Anthropic after the response is returned. — Anthropic Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

These apps are intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory reasons.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@askakshay.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@askakshay.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Microphone Access (AI Meeting Notes)

The AI Meeting Notes app requests access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of recording meeting audio within the app. Audio recordings are processed locally and/or stored in your private Firebase account. We do not access your microphone outside of active recording sessions. You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings.

9. Cookies and Tracking

These are native mobile apps and do not use browser cookies. Firebase Analytics uses a resettable device identifier for analytics. You can opt out of analytics by disabling personalised ads in your device settings (Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization on Android).

We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, via an in-app notification. Your continued use of the apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
